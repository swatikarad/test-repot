HAIR STYLES

1.Side Swept Blunt Bangs

All-one-length hair that falls below the shoulder generally is not flattering to most facial types so try experimenting with bangs if you are not a fan of layers and want to keep your locks long.

2.Long Bob

Long Bob is one of the best hair styles.This hairstyle creates a straight line down to both sides of our face which looks really nice.

3.Long Pin-up curls

There is more than one way to flatter a heart-shaped face though! Ultimately, the key to finding the perfect long hairstyle for women with heart-shaped faces is Long pin-up curls.

4.Blunt cut

This hairstyle adds subtle layers throughout the length of hair to get volume and prevent hair from falling flat at the sides which is something that any woman, with any facial shape will definitely want to avoid!

5.Long side Swept Bangs

For a more toned down version, you should definitely hold onto the side swept bangs, then just work with all of the volume that you can get naturally in your hair.

6.Bob cut

A classic short hairstyle where the ends are cut to around jaw-level and aligned close to the facial area. This style is most common among women.

7.French Twist

A hairstyle wherein the hair is twisted behind the head into a sort of bun style.

8.French Braid

9.Fringe(Bangs)

Hair that is combed to the front of the head and cut at or above the eyebrows.
